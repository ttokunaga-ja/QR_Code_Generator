## Is my Wi-Fi password sent anywhere?

No. QR codes are generated in your browser, and input values are not sent to a server.

## Do I need to include https:// in a URL?

No. In URL mode, the app automatically adds `https://` when the protocol is missing.

## Can I create a QR code for an open Wi-Fi network?

Yes. Enter the SSID and leave the password field empty to generate a Wi-Fi QR code without a password.

## Can I use generated QR codes commercially?

You may use QR code images generated by this app freely. You are responsible for checking the rules around the URL, network information, and distribution context included in the code.

## Where will ads appear?

The layout currently reserves ad spaces at the top, right sidebar, and lower mobile area. Before real ad delivery is added, ad size, privacy behavior, and policy text should be reviewed.
