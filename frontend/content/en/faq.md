## Is my Wi-Fi password sent anywhere?

No. Your entry is used to show the QR code and is not saved by this app.

## Do I need to include https:// in a URL?

No. If `https://` is missing, the app adds it before creating the QR code.

## Can I create a QR code from plain text?

Yes. You can turn notes, short instructions, or extra details into a QR code.

## Can I create a QR code for an open Wi-Fi network?

Yes. Enter the SSID and leave the password field empty to generate a Wi-Fi QR code without a password.

## Can I use generated QR codes commercially?

You may use QR code images generated by this app freely. Please check the URL, Wi-Fi details, or text before sharing.

## Where will ads appear?

Ads may appear at the top, in the right sidebar, and near the bottom on mobile screens.
